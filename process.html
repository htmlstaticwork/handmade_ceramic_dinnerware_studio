Behind the Clay

Alchemy of Earth

Sourcing. Throwing. Firing. Glazing. Packing.

01
The Foundation

Natural Sourcing

It all begins with the earth. We source our high-fire stoneware clay from local deposits in Devon, known for its exceptional plasticity and organic mineral content that reveals itself after firing.

  • Pure Mineral Composition
  • Sustainable Mining Practices
  • Multi-level Wedge Process
02
The Rhythm

Masterful Throwing

The hands are the primary tools. Using a classic wooden kick-wheel, the artisan centers the clay and coaxes it into its intended form. Every movement is a conversation between the potter and the material.

"Centering is a meditative state. If the potter isn't centered, the clay never will be."
03
The Transformation

Glazing & Mineral Science

Our glazes are mixed from scratch using oxides, wood ash, and feldspar. We avoid industrial chemical stabilizers, preferring the unpredictable beauty of natural mineral reactions during the final firing.

04+05

Through the Fire & Beyond

Double Firing

A 900°C bisque firing followed by a 1250°C glaze firing vitrifies the stoneware, making it non-porous, dishwasher safe, and unbelievably durable.

Sustainable Curation

Each piece is hand-dried for 48 hours before being wrapped in 100% biodegradable honey-comb paper and recycled card for its journey to your table.
