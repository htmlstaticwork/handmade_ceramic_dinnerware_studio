Billing FAQ

Why the price difference compared to factory ceramics?

Unlike mass-produced items, our pieces are hand-thrown over hours and fired twice in energy-intensive kilns. You are paying for time, specialized skill, and high-grade natural minerals.

Do you offer payment installations?

Yes, for our Studio and Full Collections, we offer split payments through Klarna or directly via 50% deposit and 50% on completion.

Is shipping included in these prices?

Standard shipping is free for all Collection Bundles. For individual a la carte orders, shipping is calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.
