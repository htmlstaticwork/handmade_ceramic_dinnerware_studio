Over 12 Years of Craft

The Hands Behind the Clay.

Terra was born from a desire to slow down. Each piece is hand-thrown in our sunny studio, where we embrace the imperfections that make handmade ceramics so special.

Founded in 2011, grown with passion.

What started as a small personal project in a damp garage has evolved into a fully functional ceramics studio. My name is Julianne, and I've spent the last 15 years perfecting the balance between clay, water, and heat.

Artisan Clay was born out of a desire for dinnerware that feels like it belongs in the palm of your hand, not just in a cabinet. Every fingerprint, every texture, and every glaze drip is intentional—a celebration of the human touch.

Today, our studio employs three local artisans and provides mentorship programs for emerging ceramicists in the community.

The Core Pillars

Honesty

We don't hide the natural color of the clay or the marks of the wheel. Honesty in material is honesty in art.

Sustainability

We recycle 100% of our clay scraps and use electric kilns powered by solar grids to minimize impact.

Durability

Functional art should LAST. Our stoneware is high-fired to over 2200°F for extreme chip resistance.
